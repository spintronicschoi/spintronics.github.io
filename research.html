Research

Our focus areas in spintronics and 2D magnetic materials

Research Topics

1. Electric Control of Magnetism

We explore how electric fields and ionic gating techniques can control magnetic states in 2D van der Waals materials. This opens up opportunities for ultra-low-power spintronic devices.

2. Spin Transport and Magnetoresistance

Our group investigates spin current transport in magnetic heterostructures, and studies tunneling magnetoresistance, non-local spin signals, and spin Hall effect in low-dimensional systems.

3. van der Waals Magnetic Heterostructures

We fabricate and study 2D magnetic stacks with tailored interfaces. By combining different materials, we design new functionalities such as chiral spin textures, topological phases, and proximity effects.
