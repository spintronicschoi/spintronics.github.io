Spintronics Group

Exploring 2D magnetism and spin transport

Welcome

We are an experimental condensed matter physics group focusing on low-dimensional magnetic materials and spintronic phenomena. Our interests include magnetic van der Waals materials, spin transport, and electric control of magnetism.

We welcome collaborations and applications from motivated undergraduate, graduate, and postdoctoral researchers. Get in touch!
