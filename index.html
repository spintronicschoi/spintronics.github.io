Quantum-material-based Spintronics

Exploring 2D magnetism and spin transport

Welcome

We are an experimental condensed matter physics group focusing on low-dimensional magnetic materials and spintronic phenomena. Our interests include van der Waals magnetic materials, quantum-material-based spintronics, electric field control of magnetism, topological spin textures, etc.

We welcome collaborations and applications from motivated undergraduate, graduate, and postdoctoral researchers. Get in touch!
